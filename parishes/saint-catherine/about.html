Our parish

Serving Halifax's West End since 1948 — home of the Franciscans of Halifax.

Founded in 1948, Saint Catherine of Siena Church serves the community of Halifax, Nova Scotia. It is part of Archdiocese of Halifax-Yarmouth · St. Francis and St. Clare of Assisi Parish.

This page introduces the parish. For the full history, clergy biographies, and church story, please visit the parish's official site: saintcatherineofsiena.ca.
