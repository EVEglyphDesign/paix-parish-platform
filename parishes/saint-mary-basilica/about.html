Our parish

Mother church of the Archdiocese of Halifax-Yarmouth — Gothic Revival, National Historic Site.

Founded in 1820, Saint Mary's Cathedral Basilica serves the community of Halifax, Nova Scotia. It is part of Archdiocese of Halifax-Yarmouth.

This page introduces the parish. For the full history, clergy biographies, and church story, please visit the parish's official site: stmcathedral.com.
